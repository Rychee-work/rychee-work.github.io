Privacy Policy

Storage Cleaner Lite respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information Collection

The app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any server.

All file scanning and analysis are performed locally on your device.

2. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage access: To display file sizes and locations on your device.
  • Usage Access (optional): To show the last-used time of installed apps in the "Unused Apps" feature.

Usage Access is used only to read last-used timestamps. The app does not track user behavior or app usage patterns.

3. File Deletion

The app does not delete any files automatically.

When you choose to delete a file, the system’s standard Android confirmation UI is used. All deletions are initiated explicitly by the user.

4. Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect limited device information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device type and OS version

This information is handled by Google in accordance with their privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

The app does not share any user data with the developer or third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
