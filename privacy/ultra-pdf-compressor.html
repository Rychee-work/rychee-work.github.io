Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application Ultra PDF Compressor.

Information Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

File Access

The app accesses PDF files stored on your device only when you explicitly select a file. All PDF processing and compression are performed locally on the device.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device-related information such as advertising identifiers in accordance with Google’s privacy policy.

Data Sharing

The app does not upload files to any server. Files are shared only when the user explicitly uses the Android system share feature.

Children

This app is not designed for children under the age of 13.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer.
