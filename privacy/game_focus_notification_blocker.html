Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Game Focus Notification Blocker ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the App.

Privacy-first summary: The App is designed to block distracting notifications during focus or gaming sessions and save blocked notifications locally on your device for later review. We do not upload your notification content to any server. We do not show ads.

1. Information We Access

The App may access the following information only when necessary to provide its core features:

  • Notification app name
  • Notification package name
  • Notification title
  • Notification text/body
  • Notification received time
  • Focus session information, such as session start time, end time, and blocked notification count
  • App rule settings, such as blocked apps and allowed apps

2. Notification Access Permission

The App requires Android Notification Access permission to detect notifications from apps you select, hide distracting notifications during Focus Mode, and save blocked notifications in a local digest.

Notification Access is used only for the features described in this Privacy Policy. The App does not use Notification Access to upload, sell, share, or externally analyze your notification content.

3. How We Use Information

The information accessed by the App is used to:

  • Block notifications from apps selected by you during Focus Mode
  • Keep important apps allowed based on your Allow List settings
  • Show a local digest of blocked notifications after a focus or gaming session
  • Display session summaries, such as blocked notification counts and focus time
  • Save your app preferences and rule settings locally on your device

4. Local Storage

Blocked notification data is stored locally on your device. This may include notification titles, notification text, app names, package names, timestamps, and session information.

You can delete your local notification history from within the App. Deleting this data removes the locally stored digest history from your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your notification content with third parties.

We do not upload your notification content to our servers. The App does not provide notification content to advertising networks, analytics services, or external AI services.

6. Advertising

The App does not show advertisements and does not use advertising SDKs.

7. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App is designed not to send notification titles, notification text, or notification content to analytics or crash reporting services.

If we add optional analytics or crash reporting in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and ensure that notification content is not included in analytics or crash reports.

8. In-App Purchases

The App may offer a one-time Pro upgrade through Google Play Billing. Purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

Google may process purchase-related data according to Google's own privacy policy and Google Play terms.

9. Sensitive Notifications

Notifications may contain sensitive personal information. For this reason, the App is designed to keep notification content on your device and avoid cloud upload, advertising SDKs, external AI processing, or webhook-style external sharing.

We recommend adding important apps, such as Phone, Messages, Clock, Calendar, emergency alerts, banking apps, payment apps, and authenticator apps, to your Allow List so they are not blocked during Focus Mode.

10. Data Retention

Blocked notification history is kept locally on your device according to the retention settings available in the App. Free users may have limited local history retention, while Pro users may have access to extended local history features.

You may delete local notification history at any time from the App settings.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

12. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect locally stored data. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. Because notification content may be sensitive, we recommend using your device's screen lock and keeping your device secure.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
