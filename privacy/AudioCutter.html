Privacy Policy

App: Audio Cutter
Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Audio Cutter handles information when you use the app. Audio Cutter is designed as a simple audio editing tool that works locally on your device.

Summary: Audio Cutter does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Audio and video files selected by you are processed on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

Audio Cutter does not collect personal information from users. The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or other personal details.

2. Audio and Video Files

Audio Cutter may access audio or video files only when you choose files from your device for editing. These files are used only to provide app features such as trimming audio, merging audio clips, adding fade effects, extracting audio from video, and exporting edited files.

Your selected files are processed locally on your device. Audio Cutter does not upload your audio files, video files, edited files, or project content to any developer-owned server.

3. Permissions

Audio Cutter may request access to files or media on your device when needed for app functionality. These permissions are used only to let you select, edit, and save audio or video files.

The app does not use file or media access permissions to collect personal data for advertising, analytics, tracking, or unrelated purposes.

4. Advertising and Analytics

Audio Cutter does not display third-party advertisements. Audio Cutter does not use third-party advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs that collect user data.

5. Data Sharing

Audio Cutter does not share your personal data with third parties. Because the app does not collect personal data, there is no personal data to sell or share.

6. Data Retention

Audio Cutter does not store your personal data on developer-owned servers. Edited files may be saved locally on your device only when you choose to export or save them. You can delete those files at any time using your device's file manager or media library.

7. Account Creation and Deletion

Audio Cutter does not require account creation. Because no account is created, there is no account deletion process within the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Audio Cutter is not specifically directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Security

Audio Cutter is designed to process selected files locally on your device. However, no method of electronic storage or processing is completely secure. You are responsible for managing and protecting files stored on your own device.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update it, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes means that you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
