Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Phone to PC WiFi Transfer ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the App.

Summary: Phone to PC WiFi Transfer is designed to help you transfer photos and videos from your Android device to your computer browser over your local Wi-Fi network. Your photos and videos are not uploaded to our servers. No account is required.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App is designed to work locally on your device and local Wi-Fi network. We do not directly collect, store, or upload the following information to our own servers:

  • Your photos or videos
  • Your files or file contents
  • Your contacts
  • Your SMS or phone call data
  • Your microphone recordings
  • Your precise location
  • Your account credentials, because no account is required

2. Information Processed Locally on Your Device

To provide the core file transfer function, the App may access and process certain information locally on your Android device, including:

  • Photos and videos selected or made available for transfer
  • Media metadata such as file name, file size, media type, and date taken
  • Thumbnails used to display media previews
  • Local network information such as your device's local IP address
  • Connection status and connected local device IP addresses while transfer is running
  • App settings such as auto-stop time, passcode setting, and display preferences

This information is used only to operate the App's local transfer features. Your media files are not sent to our servers.

3. Local Wi-Fi Transfer

When you start a transfer session, the App may temporarily run a local file server on your Android device. This allows a computer on the same Wi-Fi or local network to access the transfer page through a web browser.

Files are made available only while the transfer session is running. When you stop the transfer session, close the App, or when the auto-stop timer ends, the local transfer access is closed.

The App is intended for use on trusted private networks. You should avoid using local transfer features on public or untrusted Wi-Fi networks.

4. App Permissions

The App may request the following permissions depending on your Android version and enabled features:

  • Photos and Videos / Media Access: Used to display and transfer photos and videos selected by you.
  • Internet / Network Access: Used to run the local transfer page on your device and allow access from your computer on the same network.
  • Wi-Fi / Network State: Used to show connection information and help you connect your computer to the phone.
  • Notifications: Used to show that a transfer session is running and to help prevent unintended background access.
  • Foreground Service: Used to keep an active transfer session running more reliably while files are being transferred.

The App does not require contacts, SMS, phone call, microphone, or precise location permissions for its core transfer function.

5. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The free version of the App may display ads. If ads are included, we may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect or process certain data, such as device identifiers, advertising ID, approximate location, app interactions, crash logs, diagnostics, or other information as described in their own privacy policies.

Third-party advertising services operate independently from us. Their data collection and processing practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. In-App Purchases

The App may offer paid features, such as a Pro upgrade. Payments are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details. Google may process purchase information according to its own terms and privacy policy.

7. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App may use analytics or crash reporting tools to help improve stability, performance, and user experience. If used, these tools may collect information such as device model, Android version, app version, crash logs, diagnostics, and general app interaction events.

We do not intentionally include your file names, photos, videos, thumbnails, or file contents in analytics or crash reports.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your photos, videos, or file contents with third parties.

Data may be processed by third-party service providers only when necessary to provide features such as advertising, in-app purchases, analytics, crash reporting, or app distribution through Google Play.

9. Data Retention

The App may store settings and temporary cache data on your device. Temporary thumbnails or session data may be deleted automatically when the transfer session ends, when cache is cleared, or when the App is uninstalled.

We do not store your photos or videos on our servers.

10. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the App's local transfer features, such as passcode access, transfer status notifications, connected device display, and auto-stop options. However, local network transfer depends on your network environment. You are responsible for using the App on a trusted Wi-Fi network.

Do not start a transfer session on public or untrusted networks if you do not want other devices on that network to attempt to access your local transfer page.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, or the minimum age required in your country. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. International Users

The App may be available in multiple countries. If you use the App outside your country of residence, your information may be processed according to the privacy practices of third-party services used by the App, such as Google Play services or advertising providers.
