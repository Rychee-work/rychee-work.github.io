Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Simple Stock Count Pro handles information. Simple Stock Count Pro is an offline stock counting and inventory checklist app for small shops, small warehouses, handmade sellers, online sellers, and personal stock tracking.

Summary: Simple Stock Count Pro does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app is designed to work offline, without ads, without subscriptions, and without an account.

1. Information We Collect

Simple Stock Count Pro does not collect personal information from users.

The stock data you enter in the app, such as item names, quantities, categories, notes, stocktaking checklist status, CSV export files, and local backup files, is stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share it using your device or another app.

2. Local Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store the following information locally on your device so that the app can provide its core features:

  • Stock item names
  • Item quantities
  • Categories
  • Stocktaking checklist status
  • CSV export files created by the user
  • Local backup files created by the user

This information remains on your device. We do not upload this information to our servers.

3. How We Use Information

Locally stored app data is used only to provide the app's features, including:

  • Displaying your stock list
  • Updating item quantities
  • Organizing items by category
  • Supporting stocktaking checklist workflows
  • Creating CSV exports and local backups when requested by the user

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

If you choose to export a CSV file, create a backup, send a file, save a file to cloud storage, or share information using another app installed on your device, that action is controlled by you and may be subject to the privacy policy of the app or service you choose.

5. Ads, Analytics, and Tracking

Simple Stock Count Pro does not contain advertising.

Simple Stock Count Pro does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or cross-app tracking technologies.

6. Account and Login

Simple Stock Count Pro does not require users to create an account or log in. Because no account is created, we do not collect account registration information such as names, email addresses, passwords, or profile information.

7. Permissions

Simple Stock Count Pro may use device file access features only when necessary for user-requested actions, such as exporting CSV files or creating/restoring local backups.

The app does not access contacts, location, camera, microphone, calendar, call logs, SMS, or other unrelated sensitive data.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Since stock data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by removing items inside the app, clearing the app's data through your device settings, or uninstalling the app.

CSV files or backup files that you exported outside the app must be deleted manually from the location where you saved them.

9. Children's Privacy

Simple Stock Count Pro is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Security

We design the app to keep user-entered stock data on the user's device and to avoid unnecessary data transfer. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure. Users are responsible for protecting their own device, exported CSV files, and local backup files.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
