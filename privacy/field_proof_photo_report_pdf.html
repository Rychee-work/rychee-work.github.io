Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Field Proof Photo Report PDF ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the App.

Field Proof Photo Report PDF is designed to help users create field photo reports, inspection reports, work proof reports, before/after reports, and PDF documents using photos, notes, timestamps, location information, signatures, and related report data.

Summary: The App is designed as a local-first tool. Your photos, report notes, signatures, and generated PDF files are stored on your device unless you choose to share or export them.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require you to create an account to use the App.

We do not directly collect or store the following information on our own servers:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your photos
  • Your PDF reports
  • Your signatures
  • Your report comments or notes
  • Your precise location history

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App may store the following data locally on your device in order to provide its core features:

  • Photos added to reports
  • Report titles and report details
  • Photo comments and notes
  • Photo labels such as Before, During, After, Issue, or Fixed
  • Generated PDF reports
  • Company name and company logo that you add
  • Signature images that you create or save
  • App settings and preferences
  • Location, timestamp, address, weather, direction, or other proof data if you choose to use those features

This data is used only to create, edit, save, preview, and export your reports.

3. Photos and Files

The App allows you to take photos, select images from your device, add comments, and generate PDF reports. These photos and files are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share, export, back up, or save them outside the App.

When you share a PDF report, image, ZIP file, CSV file, or other exported file using another app, such as email, messaging, cloud storage, or file sharing apps, the handling of that file is controlled by the app or service you choose to share it with.

4. Location Information

The App may request access to your device location only when you choose to include location or proof data in a report. Location information may be used to add GPS coordinates, address information, or related proof data to your photos and PDF reports.

Location access is optional. You can create reports without enabling location access.

If location-based features are used, location information may be stored locally as part of your report data. We do not directly upload your location data to our own servers.

5. Address, Weather, and Direction Data

Some optional proof features may use your device location to display address, weather, direction, altitude, or similar information in your report.

If the App uses an external service to obtain address or weather information, your location coordinates may be sent to that service only for the purpose of retrieving the requested information. These features are optional, and reports can be created without them.

6. Camera and Photo Library Access

The App may request camera access so that you can take photos for your reports. The App may also request access to your photos or media library so that you can select images from your device.

These permissions are used only for the report creation features of the App.

7. Microphone and Voice Input

The App may allow you to use voice input to add comments faster. If voice input is provided by your device keyboard or operating system, the processing of your voice input is handled by your device, keyboard provider, or operating system service.

The App does not directly collect or store audio recordings on our own servers.

8. Purchases and Subscriptions

The App may offer paid features, subscriptions, or one-time purchases such as Pro features, watermark removal, premium templates, company logo support, or unlimited PDF exports.

Purchases are processed by the app store provider, such as Google Play. We do not directly collect or store your payment card details. Purchase status may be stored locally on your device or verified through the app store provider in order to unlock paid features.

9. Advertising

The App is intended to be a professional business utility. At this time, the App does not include third-party advertising. If advertising is added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

10. Analytics and Crash Reports

The App may use basic diagnostics or crash reporting tools in the future to improve stability and performance. If such tools are added, they may collect technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and usage diagnostics.

These diagnostics would be used only to improve the App and fix errors. This Privacy Policy will be updated if analytics or crash reporting services are introduced.

11. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your photos, report contents, PDF files, signatures, or location data with third parties unless:

  • You choose to share or export a report using another app or service;
  • It is necessary to process an app store purchase or subscription;
  • You choose to use an optional feature that requires an external service, such as address or weather lookup;
  • We are required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.

12. Data Retention and Deletion

Report data is stored locally on your device until you delete it from the App or uninstall the App. You can delete reports, photos, generated PDF files, signatures, and settings from within the App where such options are provided.

Uninstalling the App may delete locally stored app data, depending on your device and operating system settings. Files that you exported or shared outside the App may remain in the location or third-party service where you saved them.

13. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data handled by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for managing access to your device and for carefully choosing where you share or store exported reports.

14. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for business, field work, inspection, maintenance, repair, cleaning, property management, and similar productivity purposes. It is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

15. International Users

The App may be used by users in different countries or regions. By using the App, you understand that any data you choose to share with third-party apps or services may be processed according to the privacy policies and laws applicable to those services and locations.
