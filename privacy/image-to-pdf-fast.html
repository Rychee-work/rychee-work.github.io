Privacy Policy

Image to PDF Fast ("the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information Collection and Use

The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All image processing and PDF generation are performed entirely on your device.

Advertisements

Image to PDF Fast displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain information, such as device information and advertising identifiers, to show personalized or non-personalized ads, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

You can learn more about how Google handles data here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used only to capture images for PDF creation.
  • Photos / Media: Used only to select images and generate PDF files.

These permissions are required solely for the app’s core functionality and are not used to collect personal data.

Data Sharing

The app does not share any user data with third parties. Any data collected by AdMob is handled by Google in accordance with its own policies.

Children’s Information

Image to PDF Fast is not intended for children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
