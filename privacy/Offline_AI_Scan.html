Privacy Policy

App Name: Offline AI Scan
Effective Date: April 26, 2026

Offline AI Scan respects your privacy. This app is designed to process OCR data locally on your device and to minimize data collection.

Summary: Offline AI Scan does not require account registration and does not upload your scanned images or extracted text to our servers for cloud processing.

1. Information We Process

Offline AI Scan may process the following information on your device:

  • Images selected by you or captured with your device camera
  • Text extracted from images using on-device OCR
  • Titles, tags, folders, and notes created by you inside the app
  • Purchase status required to unlock premium features

2. How the App Works

The core OCR and organization features of Offline AI Scan are intended to work locally on your device. Your scanned content is stored locally unless you explicitly export or share it yourself.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your scanned images or extracted text with third parties except when necessary to provide essential platform services, such as purchase verification through the app store provider, or when you explicitly choose to export or share your files.

4. In-App Purchases

Offline AI Scan may offer a one-time premium purchase to unlock additional features. Payment transactions are handled by the app store provider. We do not receive or store your full payment card information.

5. Data Storage

Your scanned images, extracted text, titles, tags, and related app content are stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, locally stored data may be deleted unless you exported it beforehand.

6. Exported Files

If you use export features such as TXT, CSV, or PDF export, the files are saved to a location selected by you. After export, those files are managed according to the storage rules of your device and any apps or services you choose to use with them.

7. Permissions

The app may request permissions such as:

  • Camera: to capture images for OCR
  • Photos or files access: to let you select images or save exported files

These permissions are used only for app functionality requested by you.

8. Children’s Privacy

Offline AI Scan is not specifically directed to children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through cloud account systems because the app does not require user account registration for core functionality.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information by keeping core processing local to your device. However, no method of device storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by changing the effective date on this page.
