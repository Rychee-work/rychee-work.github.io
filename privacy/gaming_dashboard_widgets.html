Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gaming Dashboard Widgets ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our Android application.

Gaming Dashboard Widgets is a home screen customization app that provides gaming-style widgets such as battery widgets, clock widgets, RAM-style meters, storage-style panels, network-style panels, and neon dashboard designs.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or personal documents.

The app may store certain settings locally on your device, such as:

  • Selected widget themes
  • Widget customization settings
  • Color and transparency preferences
  • Whether onboarding has been completed
  • Pro unlock status

These settings are used only to make the app and widgets work properly. They are stored locally on your device and are not directly sent to us.

2. Widget and Device Information

To display widgets correctly, the app may access basic device status information available through Android system APIs, such as:

  • Battery level
  • Charging status
  • Current time and date
  • Storage usage information
  • Basic network connection status

This information is used only to display widget content on your device. We do not use this information to identify you personally.

3. Advertising

The free version of the app may display ads using third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

Third-party advertising providers may collect and process certain data, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, app interaction data, approximate location, diagnostics, and other data as described in their own privacy policies.

These third-party services may use this information to provide, measure, and improve advertising, including personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your settings and applicable laws.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. In-App Purchases

The app may offer a one-time Pro unlock or additional paid features through Google Play Billing.

Payment processing is handled by Google Play. We do not receive or store your credit card number, bank account information, or other payment details.

We may store purchase status locally on your device or verify it through Google Play services to enable paid features.

5. Permissions

The app is designed to use minimal permissions. Depending on the version, the app may use permissions required for basic widget functionality, network status detection, advertising, purchase verification, or restoring widgets after device restart.

The app does not require:

  • User account registration
  • Precise location access
  • Contacts access
  • Photos or media access
  • Accessibility Service access
  • VPN access
  • Root access

6. No Game Performance Boosting

Gaming Dashboard Widgets is a visual customization app. It does not boost game performance, clean RAM, modify other apps, monitor private app usage, or measure real in-game FPS.

FPS-style, ping-style, and RAM-style widgets are visual gaming-style displays and should not be understood as professional performance measurement tools.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or in-app purchases. These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Examples of third-party services that may be used include:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase services, if enabled in future versions

Please review the privacy policies of these third-party services for more information.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data with third-party service providers only as necessary to operate ads, purchases, app functionality, diagnostics, or legal compliance.

9. Data Retention

App settings stored locally on your device remain there until you reset the app, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

Data collected by third-party services is retained according to each third-party provider's own privacy policy.

10. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable laws.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

11. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect app-related information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Your Choices

You can control or delete locally stored app data by:

  • Changing widget settings in the app
  • Using reset options inside the app
  • Clearing app data from Android system settings
  • Uninstalling the app
  • Managing ad personalization settings in your Google account

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update it, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page.

You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
