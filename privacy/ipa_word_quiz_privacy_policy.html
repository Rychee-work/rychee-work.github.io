Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how IPA Word Quiz (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use the App.

IPA Word Quiz is an English learning quiz app that helps users practice reading IPA phonetic symbols by guessing English words.

This App is designed to work mainly offline. We do not require users to create an account, and we do not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal profile information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from users through the App.

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Quiz progress
  • Current streak and best streak
  • Selected IPA accent setting, such as US IPA or UK IPA
  • Selected difficulty level
  • Mistake review data
  • Daily quiz completion status
  • Audio and quiz-related settings
  • Premium purchase status

This information is used only to provide App features and improve your learning experience. It is stored locally on your device unless a third-party service used by the App processes related data as described below.

2. Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services to provide advertising, in-app purchases, and other app-related functionality. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

The App may use the following services:

  • Google AdMob for displaying advertisements
  • Google Play Billing for Android in-app purchases
  • Apple In-App Purchase if the App is released on iOS in the future

Advertising services such as Google AdMob may collect or use device information, advertising identifiers, app interactions, approximate location, diagnostics, and other data for purposes such as ad delivery, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and service improvement.

You can learn more about how Google handles data here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Advertising

The free version of the App may display ads. Ads help support the development and maintenance of the App.

Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your region, device settings, consent choices, and applicable laws.

If you purchase a premium upgrade or ad removal option, ads may be removed from the App, depending on the purchased product.

4. In-App Purchases

The App may offer in-app purchases, such as premium unlock, ad removal, additional levels, or other paid features.

Payment transactions are processed by the app store provider, such as Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not directly collect or store your payment card information.

5. Local Data Storage

The App may save data locally on your device to provide features such as streak tracking, mistake review, daily quiz progress, settings, and purchase status.

If you uninstall the App, locally stored data may be deleted from your device.

6. Audio Files

The App may include pronunciation audio files. These files are used only for language learning features. Audio playback does not require recording your voice.

The App does not use the microphone and does not record, collect, or upload your voice.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general English learning audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data only when necessary through third-party services used by the App, such as advertising and app store payment processing services, as described in this Privacy Policy.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information handled by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

10. Your Choices

Depending on your device and region, you may be able to:

  • Reset or limit your advertising identifier
  • Disable personalized ads through device settings
  • Manage app permissions from your device settings
  • Delete local App data by clearing storage or uninstalling the App
  • Restore purchases through the app store account used for purchase

11. International Users

The App may be used by users in different countries and regions. Third-party services used by the App may process data in countries outside your country of residence, subject to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Continued use of the App after changes to this Privacy Policy means that you accept the updated policy.
