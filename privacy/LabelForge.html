Privacy Policy for LabelForge

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Summary: LabelForge is designed as an offline label creation app. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not intentionally collect or sell personal information. Content that you enter to create QR codes, barcodes, labels, images, or PDF files is processed on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how LabelForge ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the App. LabelForge helps users create QR codes, barcodes, text labels, image labels, and printable PDF label sheets.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account and we do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, or payment card information.

The App may process the information you enter into labels, QR codes, barcodes, notes, URLs, item names, or templates. This information is processed locally on your device to create labels and output files.

3. Local Storage and Files

LabelForge may store app data locally on your device, such as label history, templates, settings, generated images, and PDF files. This data remains on your device unless you choose to export, share, print, delete, or otherwise transfer it.

You can delete locally stored data by using available in-app delete functions, clearing the App's data from your device settings, or uninstalling the App.

4. How We Use Information

Information entered into the App is used only to provide the App's core features, including:

  • Generating QR codes and barcodes
  • Creating text labels and label templates
  • Saving label history and user settings on your device
  • Exporting labels as image or PDF files
  • Sending labels to Android print or share features when you choose to do so

5. Sharing and Exporting

LabelForge does not automatically send your label content to us or to third parties. If you choose to share, print, upload, or open generated files using another app or service, that action is controlled by you and may be subject to the privacy policy of the selected app or service.

6. Third-Party Services

The App is intended to work without advertising or third-party analytics SDKs. If the App is distributed through Google Play, purchases, refunds, licensing, and related store functions may be processed by Google Play according to Google's own terms and privacy policy.

We do not sell personal information and we do not use the App to track users across apps or websites.

7. Permissions

Depending on the features you use and your Android version, the App may request limited permissions or use system features for saving files, opening files, sharing files, or printing labels. These permissions are used only to provide the feature you select.

8. Data Retention

Because label content and generated files are stored locally, they remain on your device until you delete them, clear the App's data, or uninstall the App. We do not maintain a server-side copy of your label content.

9. Security

We design the App to process label data locally and to avoid unnecessary data transmission. However, no device, app, or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Please avoid storing highly sensitive information in QR codes, barcodes, or labels unless you understand the risks.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can review the issue.

11. Your Choices

You control the information you enter into the App. You may choose not to enter personal information into labels or QR codes. You may also delete local App data or uninstall the App at any time.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
