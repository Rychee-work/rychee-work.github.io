Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Phonics Drill handles user information. AI Phonics Drill is designed as an offline phonics practice app for Android.

1. Information We Collect

AI Phonics Drill does not collect personally identifiable information.

The app does not require:

  • Account registration
  • Email address
  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contact information

2. Audio Data

AI Phonics Drill may use the microphone for pronunciation and phonics practice. Audio is processed on the device for practice and evaluation purposes.

The app does not upload user audio to any server. The app does not share user audio with third parties.

3. Offline Processing

AI Phonics Drill is designed to work offline. Core learning features are processed on the device. User practice data is stored locally on the device only.

4. Learning Data

The app may store learning progress locally, such as:

  • Completed exercises
  • Practice history
  • Weak phonics rules
  • Accuracy statistics
  • Streak and progress information

This data remains on the user's device and is not transmitted to us.

5. Advertising

AI Phonics Drill does not display advertisements. The app does not use advertising SDKs. The app does not use the Android Advertising ID.

6. Analytics

AI Phonics Drill does not use third-party analytics services.

7. Third-Party Services

AI Phonics Drill does not use cloud-based speech recognition services for core pronunciation practice. The app does not send user audio or practice data to external servers.

8. Children’s Privacy

AI Phonics Drill may be used by children for English phonics practice. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. No account registration is required, and no personal data is requested.

9. Data Deletion

Since learning data is stored locally on the device, users can delete app data from Android system settings or by uninstalling the app. If the app provides an in-app reset option, users may also clear progress from within the app.

10. Permissions

AI Phonics Drill may request microphone permission to allow pronunciation practice. The microphone is used only for practice and on-device evaluation.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
