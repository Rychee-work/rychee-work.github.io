Privacy Policy

App: Tech Word Radar

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tech Word Radar handles information when you use the app. Please replace the contact information and developer name below before publishing this page.

1. Overview

Tech Word Radar is an Android app that helps users discover trending technology words from public technology articles, developer news, blogs, RSS feeds, and public APIs. The app displays technology words as a visual word cloud and ranking, and lets users search the meaning of a word, open related articles, save words or articles locally, and share prompts to AI assistant apps using the Android share sheet.

We designed the app to work without user accounts and without requiring users to submit personal information to us.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide your name, email address, phone number, or other directly identifying information to use the core features of the app.

2.2 Information stored locally on your device

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Saved technology words
  • Saved article links
  • Search history, if enabled
  • App settings, such as selected article sources, theme, update interval, and cache settings
  • Cached article metadata, such as article titles, source names, URLs, publication dates, and short descriptions

This information is stored on your device to provide app functionality. We do not operate a server for syncing this information in version 1.x of the app.

2.3 Automatically processed information

The app may process article titles, summaries, tags, source names, and publication dates from public RSS feeds or public APIs. This processing is used to extract technology words and calculate local trend scores on your device.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

In the core app functionality, we do not collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your precise location
  • Your contacts
  • Your photos, videos, or files
  • Your messages
  • Your payment card information
  • Account credentials for third-party AI services

4. How We Use Information

Information processed by the app is used for the following purposes:

  • To fetch public technology articles and developer news
  • To extract trending technology words from article metadata
  • To display a word cloud and trend ranking
  • To show related article cards for a selected technology word
  • To save words and articles locally for later access
  • To open web searches and article links in your browser
  • To create share text for AI assistant apps or other apps selected by you
  • To remember app preferences and settings

5. Public Article Sources and External Links

The app may fetch article metadata from public sources such as RSS feeds, public APIs, developer blogs, and technology news sources. The app displays article cards that may include the article title, source name, publication date, short description, matched technology words, and a link to the original article.

When you open an article, you leave the app and visit an external website. External websites are operated by third parties and are governed by their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of external websites.

6. AI Assistant Sharing

The app may allow you to share a generated prompt, selected technology word, article title, or article URL to another app through the Android share sheet. You choose which app receives the shared content.

We do not directly send your data to ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, or any other AI service unless you explicitly choose to share content with such an app. Once shared, the receiving app or service handles the content according to its own privacy policy.

7. Advertising

The app may display ads, such as banner ads or interstitial ads, to support free access to the app. If ads are enabled, third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob may collect and process certain information, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app interaction information, diagnostics, and other data as described by their own policies.

You can learn more about Google’s privacy practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

If a paid “Remove Ads” or “Pro” option is offered, users may choose to remove ads through an in-app purchase where available.

8. In-App Purchases

The app may offer optional paid features, such as removing ads or unlocking Pro features. Payments are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With third-party services that are necessary to provide app functionality, such as advertising or app store services
  • When you choose to open an external website or share content to another app
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the app, or others

10. Data Retention

Locally stored data, such as saved words, saved articles, app settings, and cached article metadata, remains on your device until you delete it, clear the app cache/data, use an in-app deletion feature, or uninstall the app.

Data processed by third-party services, such as advertising providers or external websites, is retained according to those services’ own policies.

11. Permissions

The app may request the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET: Used to fetch public article metadata, open web searches, load external links, and display ads if enabled.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check network availability before attempting to fetch data.

The app does not require permissions for contacts, precise location, camera, microphone, photos, videos, or files for its core functionality.

12. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for a general developer and technology-interested audience and is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum age required by applicable law in your country. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

13. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information handled by the app. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

14. Your Choices

You can control your data in the following ways:

  • Delete saved words and saved articles inside the app, if this feature is available
  • Clear the app cache or app data from Android settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from your device
  • Choose not to share content through the Android share sheet
  • Use device or Google settings to manage ad personalization where available

15. International Users

The app may be used by users in different countries. Third-party services used by the app, such as advertising providers, app store services, external websites, or AI assistant apps selected by you, may process information in countries other than your own. Their processing is governed by their respective privacy policies.
