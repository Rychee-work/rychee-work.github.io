1. Overview

LocalUpscaler is an Android app that enhances and upscales images directly on the user's device. Image processing is performed locally on the device. We do not upload your images to our servers.

The App accesses images that you explicitly choose for upscaling or enhancement. These images are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

This data is used only to provide the App’s functionality.

2.3 Purchase Information

If the App offers a one-time purchase or other in-app products, payment processing is handled by Google Play. We do not collect or store your credit card or payment details. Payment-related information is handled according to Google’s policies and terms.

We use the information handled by the App only for the following purposes:
